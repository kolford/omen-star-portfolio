# Omen Star Ltd. — Portfolio & Production Archive

A collection of immersive experiences, chamber larps, and blockbuster events created by **Omen Star**, a London-based larp collective founded in 2019.

**Founder & Creative Director:** Kol Ford

🔗 **Official Website:** [omenstar.com](https://omenstar.com)

---

## About Omen Star

Omen Star are makers and creators of chamber larps, immersive experiences, and blockbuster events [citation:5]. Based in London, United Kingdom, the collective focuses on transformative play, narrative architecture, and accessible larp design [citation:5].

**Founded:** 2019 [citation:5]
